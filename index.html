About Me

Hello! I'm a 19-year-old tech enthusiast and student from Vietnam, diving headfirst into the world of coding, app development, and gaming. Ever since I discovered the world of programming, I've been captivated by the endless possibilities it offers to create, innovate, and solve problems. My journey into tech started small, with basic coding projects, but quickly grew into a passion for designing websites, developing applications, and even creating bots that do interesting things.

Currently, I'm honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, working on building websites that reflect my style and functionality ideas. It's thrilling to learn how each line of code brings a page to life, and I’m fascinated by how different technologies connect to create user-friendly experiences. Not only does web development allow me to creatively express ideas, but it’s also my way of bringing my thoughts and dreams into something tangible on the internet.

Alongside web development, I've dabbled in various programming languages such as Python, Java, and PHP. Each one has offered me unique insights and tools that I can use in my future projects. Python, for instance, has helped me understand data structures and algorithms, while Java has given me a taste of object-oriented programming—a skill I plan to build upon as I explore more complex coding challenges and even game development.

I'm also a huge fan of video games, especially Minecraft. I love tinkering with it, understanding how it works, and creating my own stuff like plugins and mods. Minecraft is like a dream playground for developers like me.

So, if you're up for it, let's journey together into the exciting world of coding and gaming. There's so much to explore, and I'm just getting started!
